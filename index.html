Our Values

At Belgrac, we are guided by principles that allow us to excel in our work. Our core values encompass the character of Belgrac. They underscore our commitment to people, professionalism, and accountability. They define us as a company and delineate where we are not willing to compromise.

We are committed to the continued development of our own internal safety as well as the safety of our clients on-site.

Belgrac is committed to total customer satisfaction. We pledge to meet or exceed customer expectations on every project.

Customers, contractors, vendors, and suppliers will be able to count on Belgrac management and personnel to deal straightforwardly and adhere to the strictest moral and ethical codes of business conduct.

What We Do

Domestic Electrician

From simple maintenance and repairs to full system design and specification. We work on single homes of all sizes to multi-home estates.

Commercial Electrician

We cover all types of electrical services for commercial premises from basic repairs and maintenance to full power, lighting and electrical systems.

CCTV & Alarm Systems

We offer turnkey system design, specification, installation and set-up services for commercial CCTV & Alarm Systems.

Belgrac Electrical Supplies

We stock and supply electrical supplies of all major brands allowing us to offer electrical supplies to all areas of the building industry and commercial sector.

Industrial Electrician

We can design a new system or work with existing plans and on-site contractors such as plumbers, project managers and engineers to ensure all installations run smoothly.

Belgrac Solar

Alongside installation of Solar PV systems, we also provide maintenance and repair services such as fault fixing and performance improvements of older systems.
